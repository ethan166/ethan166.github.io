All About A Guy Who Loves Burmese Cuisine

My full Burmese name is Kyaw Myo Tun. But I usually go by Ethan since there is no way to call my Burmese name correctly in English pronounciation, for example K has J sound in Burma. I graduated from San Francisco State University with a Computer Science Degree in 2019. San Francisco Bay Area is the place where I first landed when I moved to United States in 2014 and I immediately fell in love with diversity of people, culture and food. As a Burmese food lover myself, I explored around Bay Area in search of authentic Burmese cuisine and created a website about Bay Area Burmese cuisine since Bay Area is also known for their famous Burmese restaurants as I wanted to share my research with people who wants to try out food from different culture. A wise man once said that "If you are visiting Bay Area, you've got to try Burmese Food." Love what he said and I could not agree more.
