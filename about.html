About a guy who loves Burmese Cuisine

My full Burmese name is Kyaw Myo Tun. But I usually go by Ethan since there is no way to call my Burmese name correctly in English pronounciation. I am currently a SFSU student.I received an Associated Degree from CCSF in 2017. I wanted to create a website about Burmese cuisine since Bay Area is known for their famous Burmese restaurants. I once heard that from a guy that "If You Are Visiting Bay Area, You've Got To Try Burmese Food."
