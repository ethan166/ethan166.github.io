Popular Burmese Dishes

Palatha

Pan Fried Pancake served with chicken coconut curry. It could be served with served cooked yellow beans or just sugar as it comes in different varieties.

Ohn Noh Kauk Swe

Also known as coconut noodle. Coconut flavored chicken curry soup usually served with medium-sized round wheat noodle. A great combo for summer!

Moh Hin Kar

Fish Soup served with thin rice noodles. A warming dish suitable for cold weather.

Chicken Curry with Potatoes

Chichen curry cooked in Burmese style.

Toe Huu Nwe

A native noodle dish influneced by a ethnic group called Shan.

Khayan Chinthi Chet

Burmese Tomato Curry, a great side dish usually served with fresh vegetables such as steamed okara, and fresh cucumber

Nan Pyar

Nan bread served with cooked yellow peas. A traditional Burmese snack usually accompanied by tea or coffee.
